Language Courses

Enrolments to language courses will take place on the same days as the Club registration. The respective forms must be filled out which are available at the office (outside on the right) or can be downloaded here. New timetables and many other information about the courses will be available in the office, on the website and on the notice board close to our office. Language courses will start on 1st October 2015.

For further information please contact: Fiona McConnochie corsi@clubeuropeo.it

To attend the courses you need to fill in the application form and take it to the office. We will fix a maximum number of students for each course. If the course reaches the maximum number of students permitted, you will be put on a waiting list. Everyone will have the opportunity to attend one or more courses, or to be on a waiting list if the preferred course is over-subscribed.

Classes will begin on 1st October 2015 and end by 30th June 2016. The language courses normally follow the calendar of the European School in Varese. Teachers will inform their students of any changes to their classes. Language courses take place in the Club House, Via Esperia, Ispra.
